About TBC x GeoLab

This website is a student project created as part of the collaboration between TBC Bank's Education Initiative and GeoLab. Its goal is to demonstrate modern, accessible web development in an educational context.

Although this site is not commercially active, it represents the mission of TBC and GeoLab: blending innovation, technology, and creativity to empower learners in Georgia and beyond.
